Bayesian Reading Group - Bayes workshop
     

Bristol Bayes for the Brain Workshop


14 / 15 June 2022 - Study Group

Overview: For this study group we will consider a couple of example data sets from neuroscience and work together on building a Bayesian model for those data; it will be a chance to talk about Bayesian modelling and to hear each others ideas and insights; basically the plan is to sit around in mixed-background, mixed experience groups and chat about Bayesian modelling, with examples.

Data

  • ASJP: Word lists in different languages:
    • asjp.clld.org
    • zenodo download
  • EEG for BCI: EEG data for mental imagery: Scientific Data
  • Reading time data for text: Frank et al.
  • Some neuropixel data: figshare
